How FitPockets Works

Automated Pay Schedules

Set up custom pay schedules that automatically organize your income. Whether you're paid weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly, FitPockets adapts to your rhythm and ensures your money is allocated efficiently.

Bill Tracking

Never miss a payment again. Track all your bills in one place with automatic reminders and due date management. Stay ahead of your financial obligations with clear visibility into upcoming expenses.

Household & Dual-Income Support

Designed for modern families. Manage multiple income sources, shared expenses, and household budgets seamlessly. FitPockets helps couples and families coordinate their finances with transparency and ease.

Financial Health Metrics

Monitor your financial fitness with real-time metrics and insights. Track your spending patterns, savings progress, and overall financial health to make informed decisions and achieve your goals.

Important: FitPockets is a mobile application designed to help you manage your finances. FitPockets is not a bank, does not hold deposits, and does not provide banking services. Your funds remain in your existing bank accounts.
