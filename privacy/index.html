Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

FitPockets ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

To provide you with our financial management services, we collect the following types of information:

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and any other information you provide during registration.

Financial Information

We collect information about your income, bills, expenses, and financial transactions. This information is necessary to provide you with automated pay schedules, bill tracking, and financial health metrics.

Bank Account Information

To connect your bank accounts, we use third-party services (such as Plaid) that securely authenticate and access your account information. We do not store your bank account credentials directly. All bank account connections are handled through secure, encrypted third-party services that comply with industry standards.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with the FitPockets app, including features you use, time spent in the app, and technical information such as device type, operating system, and app version. This helps us improve our services and troubleshoot issues.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our financial management services
  • Process your transactions and manage your pay schedules
  • Send you notifications about bills, payments, and account activity
  • Generate financial health metrics and insights
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

Data Sharing and Disclosure

FitPockets does not sell your personal data. We do not rent, trade, or sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party providers to deliver our services, including:
    • Plaid and similar financial data aggregation services for bank account connections
    • Cloud hosting providers for data storage and processing
    • Analytics services to understand app usage and improve performance
    • Customer support platforms to assist with your inquiries
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure data transmission, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of certain communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

Children's Privacy

FitPockets is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.
